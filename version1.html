Unique Value

Our product brings a unique solution to a stressed-out population with (potential) mental health issues. Not only can users receive convenient and professional treatments from this virtual world, but also they will have the opportunity to experience this colorful world without worrying about being rejected like in the real social environment.

Currently, no product or platform offers solutions for mental health problems while providing a cure for their deeper and inner problems of social avoidance.

Brand New Treatment Form

Thrivo is a new form of treatment for people with these mental phobias and disabilities. We help people get good treatment without them having to leave their homes.

People can experience inexpensive treatment with just a VR headset. The Thrivo platform provides them with the right mobile entrance for another metaverse world full of similar people, professional therapists, and abundant part-time jobs to make them feel valued.
